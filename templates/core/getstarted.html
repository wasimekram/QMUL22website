Development of low cost education robot and materials

We propose to create a two-link manipulator robot and utilise it in conjunction with an augmented reality display.

About the project

The system will consist of a two-link actuated mechanism with an embedded real-time controller and a computer screen for colocated augmented reality (AR) display.
